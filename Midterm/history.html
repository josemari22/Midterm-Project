HISTORY

Eastwood College of Science and Technology

EASTWOODS International Institute of Science and Technology, was the 3rd institution founded in Bataan by the President Mr. Gregorio M. Sison Jr, where the first was EASTWOODS Professional College of Science and Technology (formerly SOFTNET College) in Balanga City founded in 1994, and second was SOFTNET Information Technology Center in Mariveles founded in 1997. They come up with the idea of building a branch in Dinalupihan because of the big percentage of drop-out students due to transportation problem. It was on November 30, 2005, the school registered to SEC (Security and Exchange Commission). It started its operation on January 2006 with TESDA courses 2- Year Computer Science in Programming, 2-Year Computer Technician, 2- Year HRM, 2- Year Practical Nursing.

During the first year of operation, it already hit 196 students renting the 3rd and 2nd Floor of the 3-storey Masagana Bldg. On 2007, its second year of operation, EASTWOODS - Dinalupihan increased the number of enrollees to 434 students and already occupied the whole building. Last April 2008 marked the First Commencement Exercise at Louis Restaurant with 111 graduates.



In year 2009, Computer Secretarial has been offered. In the following year, Tourism course has been offered.

The school name, “EASTWOODS International Institute of Science and Technology” has now known as EASTWOODS College of Science and Technology on February 14, 2012.
